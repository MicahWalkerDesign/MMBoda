### 🏛️ Theme Implementation Strategy

To keep the "Glassmorphism" look you want for your wedding web app while using these specific colors, here is how to apply them professionally:

#### 1. The Glass Backgrounds

Instead of using pure white for your translucent layers, use a tint of your background color to maintain the warmth of the site.

* **Card Background:** `rgba(251, 245, 236, 0.7)` (70% opacity Bone)
* **Border/Stroke:** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.4)` (Adds that "frosted edge" highlight)

#### 2. Visual Hierarchy

* **Headings:** Use **Terracotta** for `h1` and `h2` tags. It feels established and welcoming.
* **Call to Actions (CTAs):** Use **Fuchsia** for buttons (like "Upload Photo" or "RSVP"). It stands out perfectly against the neutral background.
* **Secondary Elements:** Use **Sage** for icons or small decorative chips (e.g., "September 25, 2026").

#### 3. Typography Pairings

Your CSS already specifies a great pairing:

* **Headlines:** `Poppins` (Clean, geometric, modern).
* **Body Text:** `Inter` (Highly readable, professional, and scales well on mobile).
